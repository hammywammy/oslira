Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans at any time?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any differences in your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my credit limit?

If you approach your monthly credit limit, we'll notify you via email. You can either upgrade your plan or purchase additional credits as needed. We'll never cut off your service unexpectedly.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a free plan with 25 monthly credits so you can test our platform. Additionally, all paid plans come with a 14-day money-back guarantee.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties or hidden fees. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
